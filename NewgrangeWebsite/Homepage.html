    Connect with History

    Newgrange is an old passage tomb located in the Boyne Valley, Ireland’s Ancient East, in Co. Meath, Ireland. The passage tomb is 5200 years old, making it older than the pyramids in Egypt. Other surrounding sites include Knowth, a Neolithic passage grave and Dowth a Neolithic passage tomb. Dive into this incredible aspect of Irish History with us today!
