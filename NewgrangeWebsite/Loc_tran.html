    Location and transportation

    Location

    Newgrange is located in Donore, County Meath which is just above Dublin. Therefore, it would be easy to visit Newgrange.

    (the Boyne Village Map.jpg, from Pinterest https://www.pinterest.ie/pin/14636767514107427/)

    Transportation from Dublin to Newgrange

    You can first take the bus 100X from Trinity College Dublin to Dundalk Bus Station Long Walk. Then you need to walk for 15 minutes to the Drogheda Bus Station to take bus 163 to Newgrange Visitor Centre. The whole journey takes about 2 hours.

    For other areas, please check the Transport For Ireland Website. https://www.transportforireland.ie/plan-a-journey/

    Drive

    If you drive from Dublin to Newgrange, you can take the M1, M2 or M3 highway to go to Newgrange, all of them take less than an hour to visit.
