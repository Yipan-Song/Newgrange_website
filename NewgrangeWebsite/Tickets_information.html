    Tickets information

    There are three types of tickets that you can choose, they are Bru na Boinne Tour, Bru na Boinne Plus Newgrange Chamber Tour and Visitor Centre Exhibition. Details are below.

    *People at the age of 60 or above are recognized as seniors.
    *Children under 12 are free to enter any tour but they need to get free tickets from the visitor centre.
    *Children at the age of 12-17 need to pay for entry.
    *Family package includes 2 adults and up to 5 children.

    Customer service:

    If you have any question about booking, refund or other inquiry, please call our hotline, email us nosuchemail@newgrange.ie, or leave us a message below.
